Program reference¶

dhf¶

Electric field gradients, nuclear quadrupolar coupling and Mossbauer spectroscopy for relativistic 4-component DHF and DKS methods. (In testing)

rhf¶

Electric field gradients, nuclear quadrupolar coupling and Mossbauer spectroscopy for non-relativistic (or sf-x2c) mean-field and post-HF methods. (In testing)

Ref:
